Game Play

Goal
Be the first player to reach field number 30.

How to play
On the right side of the board, you have a panel that shows an overview of whos turn it is. You can see whos turn it is by looking at the header or check if there is a dice under your token. You then start your turn by clicking the roll dice button.

Orange Fields
The orange fields are cardfields and if you land on one of these fields you will automatically be delt a card. This card can either move you forward, backward or make you stay in your current position.

PS: the orange fields change position for every new game you start.

Good Luck!
